# Coordination

> Civilization is not built by intelligence alone. It is built by coordination.

Humans are often described as the most intelligent species on Earth.

Semut begins with a different observation.

An intelligent individual can survive.

A coordinated society can build civilizations.

Language, culture, institutions, markets, science, and technology all emerge because people coordinate across space, time, and generations.

Coordination allows knowledge to accumulate instead of being rediscovered.

Coordination allows individuals to contribute to something greater than themselves.

Intelligence makes coordination possible.

Coordination makes civilization possible.

This essay explores coordination as one of humanity's defining characteristics and asks what role artificial intelligence should play within it.

## Topics

- Why coordination matters
- Coordination versus intelligence
- Collective memory
- Language and culture
- Institutions as persistent coordination
- AI as a coordination partner
- Open questions
